# Gritty grimdark

Mörk Borg. Warhammer Fantasy. Soulslike. The sun has set, the saints have died, and your character is going to die too. The aesthetic should *feel* heavy.

## Ambiance adjectives

- oppressive, claustrophobic
- iron-and-soot, blood-stained, ash-flecked
- woodcut, scratched, distressed
- nocturnal — the world after dusk
- monastic in a doomed way
- punctured, scarred, scab-edged

## Mature-tool style refs

- Mörk Borg's official rulebook (typography, color violence)
- Dark Souls UI (material weight, restraint)
- Bernie Wrightson's woodcuts (linework)

## Color/material palette (in prose)

Surfaces are `ink-900` near-black or a deep bone color (`#d8c8a8`) with heavy distress. The dominant accent is `danger-500` arterial red — used for emphasis, never as a default chrome color. Bone, blood, and rust. Avoid jewel tones; this aesthetic doesn't have a `rune-blue` mood.

Texture: heavy paper grain, occasional stains, scratched borders. Borders are blocky and irregular — woodcut-style, not engineered.

## Typographic intent

- Display: a brutal display face — IM Fell English DW Pica, or a woodcut-style face. Heavy.
- Body: a slab serif or a no-nonsense humanist serif at higher contrast.
- Mixed sizes within a single column for emphasis (Mörk Borg style) — but only when the brief explicitly leans into it.
- Numerals: lining only. No oldstyle.
- Tracking: chaotic, deliberate. Some words inverted on a black bar for maximum hierarchy.

## Motion intent

- Minimal. The aesthetic is heavy; flourish is wrong.
- Page transitions: cut to black (50ms blackout), then reveal.
- Hover: a small ink-blot expansion, no glow.
- Dice: weighted, slow roll, low-bounce settle.
- Damage feedback: brief vignette darken, no shake.

## Anti-patterns

- **Glow / candlelight effects.** This is the wrong aesthetic for warmth. If a brief asks for "atmospheric" while in this key, atmosphere = darkness, not glow.
- **Smooth gradients.** Texture is the gradient.
- **Centered mid-weight typography.** Asymmetric, brutal, off-balance is on-brand.
- **Bright primary accents.** Red is the primary; everything else is bone, soot, or grease.
